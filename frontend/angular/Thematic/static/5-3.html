Domain 13: Contingency planning

Finally, successful scaling up will call for appropriate contingency planning, or procedures to maintain the continuity of use of the product in the event of infrastructure or programme obstacles. First, procedures must be established to address technical constraints related to connectivity and electricity (see Axis 1: Groundwork), for users to operate the application during network shortages, power outages or other unforeseen issues. In addition, promoting the retention of devices requires strategic planning and the adoption of procedures to prevent loss, theft, breakage and the misuse of mobile devices. In many settings, it will be necessary for these procedures to be vetted by the community or other local stakeholders to ensure they are appropriate.
